Random Observations

  • BART escalators - some BART station exits have only one escalator (19th St. Oakland is an example). The natural question is should the escalator go up or down. Energy-wise, it should always go up, since its harder for people to go up a flight of stairs than to go down. But Catch-the-train-wise though, it probably makes sense for the escalator to go down because people are always rushing for the train. Of course this is dependent on the time of day.
  • Spiral Staircases - ever notice how spiral staircases go clockwise? (meaning if you were at the top looking down on someone walking up the stairs, they would appear to go clockwise). I think it's because most people are right-handed and the convention is people walk on the right-side. This way, you can hold onto the side rail as you walk.
